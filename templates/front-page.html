Welcome

Welcome to the home of the Western Pennsylvania Meshtastic community. We're a group of radio enthusiasts building an off-grid mesh network centered around Pittsburgh, with nodes reaching into Allegheny, Westmoreland, Butler, Armstrong, Indiana, and Beaver counties.

Whether you're looking to join the network, check coverage in your area, or connect with other local operators, you're in the right place.
